## üéØ Color Usage Summary

### **Primary Actions (Buttons, CTAs)**

- **Secondary CTA:** `#111184` (Deep Blue) outline or solid
- **Tertiary/Link:** `#111184` (Deep Blue) text

## üí° Design Principles Applied

1. **Visual Hierarchy:** Deep Blue for headings, Gold for achievements, Red for actions
2. **Brand Consistency:** Blue and Red as primary brand colors throughout
3. **Readability:** High contrast ratios maintained (WCAG AA compliant)
4. **Emotional Design:** Cream for warmth, Blue for trust, Red for urgency, Gold for excellence
5. **Professionalism:** Sophisticated color combinations, not overly bright
6. **Accessibility:** All color combinations meet contrast requirements
